Project Proposal


Problem Statement

People often possess the desire and potential to contribute to their communities and causes they care about through volunteering. However, they often face challenges in finding suitable volunteer opportunities that align with their interests, schedules, and skills. To address these issues, we aim to create a user-friendly web application tailored specifically for users who like selfless volunteering, enabling them to easily discover, engage in, while fostering a sense of community and personal growth.

User Goals

Finding Suitable Volunteering Opportunities: Users want to identify volunteer opportunities that align with their interests, skills, and availability.
Application:  Users aim for a user-friendly application that simplifies the process of discovering, applying for, and participating in volunteering opportunities.
Community Engagement: They seek a platform that connects them with like-minded peers and fosters a sense of community around volunteering.

User Actions

Create a Profile: Users will start by creating a personal profile.
Discover Opportunities:  They will browse through a list of volunteering opportunities and time commitment.
Register:  Users will select an opportunity and register for it through the application.
Post Oppurtunities:  Users will post voluteering options.

Obstacles

Balancing and prioritizing user experience, enjoyable designs, intuitive functionalities and user goals without compromising on each other.
